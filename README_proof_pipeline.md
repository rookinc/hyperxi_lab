# Petrie–Decagon Chamber System: Proof Pipeline

This repository contains the computational verification pipeline used
to analyze the chamber graph arising from the Petrie decagons of the
dodecahedron.

The scripts in `scripts/` generate reports stored in
`artifacts/reports/`. Together these computations verify the main
structural claims used in the accompanying paper.

## Verified Properties

### Petrie Decagon Structure

scripts/decagon_intersection_graph.py

Verifies:

- 12 Petrie decagons
- intersection graph has
  - 12 vertices
  - degree 5
  - 30 edges
  - 20 triangles

This uniquely identifies the **icosahedral graph**.

---

### Chamber Graph Construction

scripts/chamber_graph_spectrum.py  
scripts/chamber_graph_distance_profile.py

Verifies:

- 60 vertices
- 120 edges
- degree 4
- chamber graph structure induced by decagon adjacency

---

### Automorphism Structure

scripts/chamber_graph_vertex_orbits.py  
scripts/chamber_graph_edge_orbits.py  
scripts/chamber_graph_aut_order.py

These scripts explore orbit structure and symmetry of the chamber graph.

---

### Signed 2-Lift Identification

scripts/recover_2lift_signing.py  
scripts/check_2lift_spectral_split.py

Verifies that the chamber graph is a **signed 2-lift** of a 30-vertex
quotient graph.

The quotient is identified with

L(Dodecahedron)

via edge–face flag pairing.

---

### Signing Analysis

scripts/test_orientation_sign_rule.py  
scripts/test_fiber_order_sign_rule.py  
scripts/test_sign_switching_equivalence.py

These scripts investigate geometric interpretations of the lift
signing and verify switching-equivalence of candidate rules.

---

## Resulting Structure

The computations verify the following hierarchy:

Petrie decagons (12)
        ↓

        ↓
Intersection graph = Icosahedron
        ↓

        ↓

        ↓
