\section{Explicit Edge Tables}

This appendix records the edge structure of $\Gfifteen \cong L(\mathrm{Petersen})$
together with the sign function $\sigfun$ defining the signed $2$-lift.

We fix an ordering of the $30$ edges of $\Gfifteen$, as listed below.
All subsequent references to $\sigfun$ are understood with respect to
this ordering.

The value $\sigfun(e) \in \{\pm 1\}$ indicates whether the edge $e$
lifts as a parallel edge $(+1)$ or a crossed edge $(-1)$ in the
double cover $\Gthirty \to \Gfifteen$.
