
\subsection*{Transport Specification}

The construction produces the connected tetravalent graph
\[
\mathrm{AT4val}[60,6],
\]
with quotient chain
\[
G_{60} \longrightarrow G_{30} \longrightarrow G_{15},
\qquad
G_{15} \cong L(\mathrm{Petersen}).
\]

\paragraph{Graph data}
\begin{itemize}
\item $G_{60}$: $|V|=60$, $|E|=120$, degree $4$, diameter $6$
\item $G_{30}$: $|V|=30$, $|E|=60$, degree $4$
\item $G_{15}$: $|V|=15$, $|E|=30$, degree $4$
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Signed lift}
The induced signing
\[
\sigfun : E(G_{15}) \to \{\pm 1\}
\]
has distribution
\[
+1 : 10, \qquad -1 : 20.
\]

Here $+1$ corresponds to parallel lift type and $-1$ to crossed lift type.
