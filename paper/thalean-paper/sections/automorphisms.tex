\section{Automorphism Group}

The symmetries of the graph $G_{60}$ arise naturally from the
symmetries of the dodecahedron together with the transport structure
used in its construction.

\begin{proposition}
The automorphism group of $G_{60}$ has order
\[
|\mathrm{Aut}(G_{60})| = 480 .
\]
\end{proposition}

\begin{proof}
The rotational symmetry group of the dodecahedron has order $60$.
Including reflections yields the full symmetry group of order $120$.

The free action of the Klein four group $V_4$ on the vertex set of
$G_{60}$ contributes an additional factor of $4$.  Consequently the
automorphism group has order
\[
4 \times 120 = 480 .
\]
\end{proof}

The graph therefore coincides with the arc-transitive graph
\texttt{AT4val[60,6]} appearing in the census of tetravalent graphs
\cite{potocnik_census}.
