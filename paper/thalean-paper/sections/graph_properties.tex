\section{Basic Graph Properties}

The chamber transport construction produces a connected tetravalent graph
\[
G_{60}
\]
with the following parameters:

\[
|V| = 60, \qquad |E| = 120, \qquad \deg = 4 .
\]

Computational analysis yields:

\[
\text{diameter} = 6
\]

and shell profile from a base vertex

\[
(1,4,8,16,24,6,1).
\]

\begin{figure}[h]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=0.75\linewidth]{figures/g60_graph.png}
\caption{The Thalean graph $G_{60}$ obtained from chamber transport on the dodecahedron.}
\label{fig:g60_graph}
\end{figure}

The graph contains $40$ triangles and exhibits a large automorphism group,
computed to have order $480$.
