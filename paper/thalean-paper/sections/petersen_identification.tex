\section{Identification with the Line Graph of the Petersen Graph}

A direct computational comparison shows that the quotient graph $Q_{15}$
is isomorphic to the line graph of the Petersen graph:

\[
Q_{15} \cong L(\mathrm{Petersen}).
\]

\begin{figure}[h]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=0.65\linewidth]{figures/petersen_line_graph.png}
\caption{The line graph of the Petersen graph.}
\label{fig:petersen_line_graph}
\end{figure}

The isomorphism was verified using a canonical graph comparison routine
implemented in the computational pipeline accompanying this work.
