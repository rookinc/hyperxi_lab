\section{The Quotient Graph}

Collapsing the $V_4$ orbits produces a quotient graph

\[
Q_{15}.
\]

This graph has parameters

\[
|V| = 15, \qquad |E| = 30, \qquad \deg = 4 .
\]

Computationally we obtain

\[
\text{diameter} = 3,
\]

with shell profile

\[
(1,4,8,2).
\]

\begin{figure}[h]
\centering
\includegraphics[width=0.65\linewidth]{figures/q15_graph.png}
\caption{The quotient graph $Q_{15}$ obtained by collapsing $V_4$ orbits of $G_{60}$.}
\label{fig:q15_graph}
\end{figure}

The quotient graph plays a central role in identifying the deeper
combinatorial structure of the construction.
