Scaffold chamber graph:
- 60 vertices
- 120 edges
- degree 4

- diameter 4
- multiple shell profiles

Incidence quotient graph:
- 60 vertices
- 90 edges
- degree 3
- girth 5
- diameter 9
- uniform shell profile from every vertex:
  [1,3,6,8,10,10,10,8,3,1]

Interpretation:
The scaffold graph and the real incidence quotient are distinct 60-vertex constructions.
The incidence quotient is markedly more homogeneous in distance structure.
